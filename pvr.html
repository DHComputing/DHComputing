What is a PVR?

A “PVR” (Personal Video Recorder) is the only convenient way to record TV once you've “gone digital”. Similar to Sky+ but without the subscription fee, Humax PVRs for Freeview or Freesat give all the functions of Sky+, such as the ability to pause/rewind live TV and record up to two programmes simultaneously while watching a recording. Plus many other features such as BBC iPlayer access and network access to content (such as photos) stored on your computer.
