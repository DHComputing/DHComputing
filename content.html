New Service for owners of the following Humax PVRs:-

For owners (or prospective owners) of the Humax PVR models (shown), I am now able to provide advice, setup assistance and tuition to help you get the best out of these excellent devices. The supplied manuals can be quite vague and hard to follow, which is where I can help.

  • Advice on which model is best for you.
  • Assistance in setting up your Humax PVR correctly.
  • Tuition to show how to get the best out of your Humax PVR features.
  • Networking your Humax PVR to your home Internet router without the need for unsightly cables.
  • Setting up Humax home networking for access to content (such as photos) stored on your computer for display on your TV (HDR-Fox T2 only).

A "PVR" (Personal Video Recorder) is the only convenient way to record TV once you've “gone digital”.
Similar to Sky+ but without the subscription fee, Humax PVRs for Freeview or Freesat give all the functions of Sky+, such as the ability to pause/rewind live TV and record up to two programmes simultaneously while watching a recording. Plus many other features such as BBC iPlayer access and network access to content (such as photos) stored on your computer.
