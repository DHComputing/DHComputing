About DH Computing

DH Computing is a computer services business, started in 2008 by David Hale, with over 20 years experience in the IT industry.

DH Computing specialises in the IT needs of domestic and small business customers. My mission is to provide great value IT services and a personalised friendly approach, tailored to your level of IT literacy. I now have well over a thousand satisfied customers and a large and expanding network of regular customers.

Total Honesty Policy

DH Computing is a business based on trust and honesty. I will never recommend or perform work that is not necessary and will always endeavour to find the cheapest solutions to your computer problems. DH Computing will always act in your best interests.

Impartial Advice

DH Computing does not sell computers, the upside being that completely impartial advice can be given on good deals. DH Computing is purely a service provider and has no vested interest in any products, vendors or manufacturers.
